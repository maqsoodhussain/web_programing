Yusmarg

Yusmarg is a hill station in the western part of Jammu and Kashmir. It is known for its lush green meadows, dense pine forests, and stunning views of the surrounding mountains. Yusmarg is often referred to as the 'Meadow of Jesus' and is a relatively unexplored tourist destination.

The area offers several trekking and adventure opportunities, making it a popular spot for nature enthusiasts.
