Peer ki Gali

Peer ki Gali is a beautiful mountain pass located on the Mughal Road in the Pir Panjal range, connecting Poonch and Shopian districts in Jammu and Kashmir. The pass is known for its stunning landscapes and serene environment.

It is one of the highest points on the Mughal Road and is a popular destination for travelers seeking natural beauty.
