Verinag

Verinag is a beautiful spring and garden located at the foot of the Banihal Pass in Jammu and Kashmir. It is the source of the Jhelum River and is known for its octagonal stone basin and the Mughal garden surrounding it.

The spring and the garden were developed by Emperor Jahangir and later extended by his son Shah Jahan. The site is a popular tourist destination for its historical significance and scenic beauty.
