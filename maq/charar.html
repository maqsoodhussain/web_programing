Charar-e-Sharief

Charar-e-Sharief is a historic and sacred Sufi shrine located in the Budgam district of Jammu and Kashmir. The shrine is dedicated to Sheikh Noor-ud-din Noorani, a revered Sufi saint.

The town of Charar-e-Sharief is also known for its scenic beauty and spiritual significance.
