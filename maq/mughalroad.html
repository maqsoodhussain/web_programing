Mughal Road

The Mughal Road is a historic route in Jammu and Kashmir that connects Srinagar with Jammu. It was once the main trade route used by the Mughals. The road is known for its scenic beauty, including picturesque valleys and majestic mountain views.

Traveling along the Mughal Road provides a glimpse into the rich history and cultural heritage of the region.
