Kheer Bhawani

Kheer Bhawani is a sacred Hindu temple dedicated to the goddess Ragnya Devi, located in the Tullamulla village near Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir. The temple is situated amidst a natural spring and is surrounded by beautiful Chinar trees.

The temple is an important pilgrimage site and is known for its tranquil environment and spiritual significance.
