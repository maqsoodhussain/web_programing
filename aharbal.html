Aharbal

Aharbal is a breathtaking waterfall located in the Kulgam district of Jammu and Kashmir, often referred to as the "Niagara Falls of Kashmir" due to its immense beauty and power.

This hidden gem is surrounded by dense pine forests and meadows, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

The waterfall, which cascades down from the Veshu River, offers a spectacular sight, especially during the spring and summer months.

Aharbal is also known for its trekking routes, offering adventurous paths through lush landscapes, and is a great spot for trout fishing.

The serene and untouched environment of Aharbal makes it an ideal place to relax and connect with nature.
