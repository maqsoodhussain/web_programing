Kousarnag Lake

Kousarnag, also known as Vishnupad, is a high-altitude glacial lake located in the Kulgam district of Jammu and Kashmir. It is one of the highest-altitude lakes in the Kashmir Valley, situated at an elevation of over 4,000 meters.

The lake is revered by Hindus and is considered to be a sacred site. The challenging trek to Kousarnag is filled with stunning views of snow-capped mountains and pristine landscapes.

The crystal-clear waters of Kousarnag reflect the surrounding peaks, creating a mesmerizing sight that attracts trekkers and nature lovers.

The remote and untouched location of Kousarnag offers a perfect escape for those seeking solitude and a deep connection with nature.

Kousarnag is a true hidden gem of Kashmir, offering an unforgettable experience for all who visit.
