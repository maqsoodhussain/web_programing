Gangabal Lake

Gangabal Lake, also known as Harmukh Ganga, is a pristine alpine lake located at the base of Mount Harmukh in the Ganderbal district of Jammu and Kashmir.

Considered sacred by Kashmiri Hindus, the lake is a major pilgrimage site and is visited annually by devotees.

Surrounded by majestic mountains and lush green meadows, Gangabal offers an enchanting view and is a popular trekking destination.

The trek to Gangabal is a challenging yet rewarding journey, taking adventurers through some of the most beautiful landscapes in the Kashmir Valley.

The crystal-clear waters of the lake, coupled with its serene environment, make Gangabal a perfect spot for camping, fishing, and nature photography.
